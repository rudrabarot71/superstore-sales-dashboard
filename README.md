# Superstore Sales & Profit Dashboard

A comprehensive Power BI dashboard analyzing sales performance, profitability, and customer segments of a retail Superstore — built to surface actionable business insights for data-driven decision making.

---

## Objective

Conduct a comprehensive analysis of Superstore's sales performance, profitability, and customer segments using Power BI to surface key business insights and identify optimization opportunities across regions, product categories, and customer segments.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Value |
|-----|-------|
| Total Sales | 2.30M |
| Total Profit | 286.40K |
| Profit Margin | 12.47% |
| Average Order Value | 229.86 |
| Customer Count | 793 |

---

## Charts & Insights

### 1. Revenue Trend (Area Chart)
- Revenue dipped in 2015 but rebounded strongly to 7.3M by 2017
- Signals consistent business recovery and growth trajectory

### 2. Top 10 Products by Profit (Bar Chart)
- Canon imageCLASS leads at 25K profit — nearly 3x the second-ranked product
- Profit is heavily concentrated in Technology products

### 3. Sales by Region (Bar Chart)
- West region drives 32% of total revenue, highest of any region
- South contributes the least at 0.39M — a clear growth opportunity

### 4. Profit by Segment (Donut Chart)
- Consumer segment leads at 46.83%, nearly double Home Office at 21.05%
- Corporate segment holds steady at 32.12%

---

## Interactivity & Filters

The dashboard includes dynamic slicers for Year/Quarter, Category, and Ship Mode — enabling stakeholders to slice the data across any time period, product category, or fulfillment method instantly. Every visual is cross-filtered, meaning clicking any data point automatically updates the entire report — no manual filtering required.

## Dataset

The dataset used is the popular Sample Superstore dataset containing retail transaction data including:
- Orders, Sales, Profit across 4 years (2014–2017)
- 4 Regions: West, East, Central, South
- 3 Categories: Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies
- 3 Customer Segments: Consumer, Corporate, Home Office

---

## Conclusion

Leveraging Power BI to analyze Superstore data reveals actionable insights for strategic decisions across regional expansion, product focus, and customer segment targeting. This analysis aims to drive smarter inventory, marketing, and sales decisions.
